Finding My True Happiness

A Journey Through Kabuki Syndrome, Self-Doubt, and the Power of Resilience

About the Book

This heartfelt memoir chronicles the life of a young woman living with Kabuki Syndrome. Through horseback therapy, the Special Olympics, heartbreak, self-doubt, and the search for self-love, she finds her voice, strength, and happiness.

  • 🐴 Therapeutic horseback riding that offered peace
  • 🥇 Special Olympics moments that built strength
  • 💔 Battles with bullying and heartbreak
  • 💞 Discovering love, acceptance, and inner truth

What is Kabuki Syndrome?

Kabuki Syndrome is a rare genetic disorder that impacts physical and cognitive development. Those living with it often face unique challenges—and extraordinary growth. This book shines a light on what it truly means to live with a rare condition.

From the Author

I wrote this book for the outcasts, the overlooked, and the unheard. For every person who’s ever wondered, “Am I enough?” This is proof that your voice matters—and happiness begins with self-love.

Why I Wrote This

I couldn’t find books that told my truth. So I wrote it. I wanted those like me to know they aren't alone, and to help those unlike me understand. This story is mine—but the healing belongs to all of us.
